What companies are serviceable through KingsNet and JNA?

Is KingsNet a service provider?

+

KingsNet operates as a certified JNA dealer and telecommunications solutions provider. We partner with major service providers to offer their services while providing personalized customer support, installation assistance, and comprehensive account management to ensure you get the best service experience.

What are KingsNet's execution charges?

+

Our execution charges vary depending on the specific service and provider selected. These charges cover our comprehensive support services, including consultation, installation coordination, ongoing customer support, and account management. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

Why are KingsNet's execution charges separate?

+

Execution charges are separate to maintain transparency in pricing. This structure allows us to clearly distinguish between the provider's service costs and our value-added services including personalized consultation, professional installation coordination, dedicated customer support, and ongoing account management.

How do I contact KingsNet for support?

+

You can reach our dedicated support team through our website contact form or customer portal. Our customer service representatives are available 24/7 to assist with service inquiries, technical support, billing questions, and account management. We're committed to providing prompt, professional assistance whenever you need it.
